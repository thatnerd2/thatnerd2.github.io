About

a computer scientist, mathematician, and musician

___ is written and maintained by Aaron Chan, a freshman in college who is working to obtain a dual degree in Computer Science and Statistics. His projects and interests in these areas include algorithms contests, neural networks, bayesian methods and operating systems development and theory. He also frequently wonders about the world around him - the morals thereof, what people place value in, and what makes successful people successful.

Aaron also plays the piano and the cello. He served as principal cellist during his high school years, and won multiple piano competitions performing works such as the Quatrieme Ballade by Chopin, and the Grieg Piano Concerto. In addition to solo performance, Aaron loves accompanying and frequently plays for his church meetings.
