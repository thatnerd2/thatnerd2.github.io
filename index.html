Time Capsule

In a few months, I'll be gone for 2 years. Here is where I did my best to capture the information I know, beliefs I hold, and goals I set. It's online because I think that maybe it would be useful or interesting for other people. But this library of opinions is firstly for myself. Obviously, the compilation is still in progress, and can't contain everything, much like one can't drink every drop from a firehose.

The information here is meant to be a freeze of my mind at its current state. Much of it is probably wrong. Some of it is correct now, and will be wrong later. I have an explicit belief in change, and in 2 years, my expertise might be in an earlier state of the world. That's okay.

I'm Aaron Chan, musician, programmer, and mathematician.
